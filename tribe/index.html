About B.S. International Pvt. Ltd.

Quality Garment Manufacturing Since 2017

B.S. International Pvt. Ltd. is a garment manufacturing company established on February 7, 2017. We specialize in the production of quality ready-made garments, serving customers with reliable manufacturing and consistent standards.

We manufacture a wide range of products including pants, T-shirts, jackets, hoodies, one-piece garments, and other apparel items. All products can be customized according to customer requirements in terms of design, fabric, size, and finishing.

Raw materials and fabrics are sourced from both local and international markets, depending on product specifications. At present, our production is focused on the domestic market, with a long-term plan to export our products to international markets.

By following manufacturing practices and quality control procedures inspired by garment industries in India, China, and Bangladesh, we maintain internationally recognized production standards within Nepal. Our factory is supported by over 60 skilled Nepali professionals, with strong participation of women employees, contributing to employment generation and local economic development.

B.S. International Pvt. Ltd. is committed to quality manufacturing, ethical production, and continuous improvement, with the goal of becoming a trusted garment exporter from Nepal.
