The Founder's Journey

Bharat Sharma

Founder & Chairman

Salyan, Nepal
Founded BS International in 2017
International Experience

Mr. Bharat Sharma, born in the picturesque hills of Bagchaur Municipality in Salyan District, embodies the spirit of Nepali entrepreneurship. His journey from a small-town laboratory assistant to a successful garment industry leader is truly inspirational.

Starting his career as a laboratory assistant in Bardiya, Mr. Sharma's ambition led him to seek opportunities abroad. He worked in Malaysia and Saudi Arabia, where he gained invaluable experience in international business practices and quality standards.

Upon returning to Nepal, he was determined to contribute to his homeland's economic growth. He first established BS Furnishing, gaining experience in the textile industry. This foundation paved the way for the creation of BS International Pvt. Ltd. on February 7, 2017.

Mr. Sharma personally traveled to India, China, and Bangladesh to source the best materials and learn cutting-edge manufacturing techniques. His dedication to quality and continuous improvement has made BS International a name synonymous with excellence in Nepal's garment industry.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our success

2010-2015

International Experience

Mr. Bharat Sharma worked in Malaysia and Saudi Arabia, gaining expertise in international manufacturing standards and business practices.

2015-2016

BS Furnishing Established

Founded BS Furnishing to enter the textile industry, laying the groundwork for future expansion into garment manufacturing.

Feb 7, 2017

BS International Pvt. Ltd. Founded

Officially registered BS International Pvt. Ltd. in Dhapakhel, Lalitpur, marking the beginning of our journey as a premier garment manufacturer.

2018-2020

Growth & Expansion

Expanded operations with advanced machinery, hired skilled workforce, and established supply chains with India, China, and Bangladesh.

April 2021

Trademark Registration

Successfully registered our brands TRIBE and ASCENT with the Department of Industry, Nepal, establishing our intellectual property rights.

2021-2025

Industry Leadership

Grew to 60+ employees, achieved 5000+ monthly production capacity, and became a trusted name in Nepal's garment manufacturing sector with an export vision.
