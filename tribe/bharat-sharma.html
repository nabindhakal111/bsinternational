About Bharat Sharma

Born on July 26, 1980 in Bagchaur-9, Bathan, Salyan District, located in Karnali Province, Nepal, Bharat Sharma's journey is a testament to determination, resilience, and unwavering commitment to his homeland.

Starting his career as a laboratory assistant in Bardiya, Bharat recognized early on that true growth required expanding his horizons. His journey took him to Malaysia and Saudi Arabia, where he gained invaluable international experience in business operations and quality standards.

Upon returning to Nepal, Bharat was driven by a singular vision: to contribute to Nepal's economic growth while creating employment opportunities for fellow Nepalis. He first established BS Furnishing, gaining crucial experience in the textile industry before founding BS International Pvt. Ltd. on February 7, 2017.

His dedication to excellence led him to personally travel to India, China, and Bangladesh to source the finest materials and learn cutting-edge manufacturing techniques. Under his visionary leadership, BS International has grown to become one of Nepal's premier garment manufacturing companies.

Today, BS International stands as a testament to his vision, employing over 60 skilled professionals and producing world-class garments that showcase Nepali craftsmanship on the global stage. Bharat's journey from the hills of Salyan to establishing an international company exemplifies the entrepreneurial spirit of Nepal.

As Founder and Chairman, Bharat continues to guide the company with wisdom gained from decades of experience, ensuring that BS International remains committed to quality, innovation, and contributing to Nepal's economic development.
