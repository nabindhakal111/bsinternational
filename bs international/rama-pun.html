About

As the Marketing Head of BS International Pvt. Ltd., Rama Pun brings creativity, strategic thinking, and deep market insights to the company's growth trajectory.

With extensive experience in brand development, customer relations, and market positioning, she has been instrumental in establishing BS International as a trusted name in Nepal's garment manufacturing sector.

Her leadership focuses on building strong customer relationships, developing effective marketing campaigns, and expanding the company's reach both domestically and internationally.
