The Journey

Born in the picturesque hills of Bagchaur Municipality, Salyan District, Bharat Sharma's story is one of determination, resilience, and unwavering commitment to his homeland.

Starting his career as a laboratory assistant in Bardiya, he recognized early on that true growth required expanding his horizons. His journey took him to Malaysia and Saudi Arabia, where he gained invaluable international experience in business operations and quality standards.

Upon returning to Nepal, Bharat was driven by a singular vision: to contribute to Nepal's economic growth while creating employment opportunities for fellow Nepalis. He first established BS Furnishing, gaining experience in the textile industry before founding BS International Pvt. Ltd. on February 7, 2017.

His dedication to excellence led him to personally travel to India, China, and Bangladesh to source the finest materials and learn cutting-edge manufacturing techniques. Today, BS International stands as a testament to his vision, employing over 60 skilled professionals and producing world-class garments.
